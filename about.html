About Shiba Café

Welcome to the Shiba Café, where the coffee is hot and the pups are even hotter! This cozy little spot is filled with the cutest Shiba Inus you've ever seen. They'll greet you with wagging tails and adorable grins, and they're always up for a cuddle or two. As you sip your latte, you'll be surrounded by a sea of fluffy, pointy-eared dogs, each one more lovable than the last. Some will be snoozing on the couch, while others will be playing with their toys or begging for a treat. So come on down to the Shiba Café, where every cup of coffee comes with a side of puppy love. It's the perfect place to unwind, relax, and forget all your troubles, even if only for a little while.

Things to look forward to during your visit!

  • Sit down and enjoy being surrounded by the cutest shibas!
  • Free dog treats to feed our furry friends!
  • Unlimited love from shibas roaming our Café!
  • Watching the Shibas play together and take in the cuteness!
